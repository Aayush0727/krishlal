ABOUT US

Click Here and Follow Us for more informative videos and posts.

KrishLal is India's most trusted farming app for services and products. It provides a location based platform for farmers to help them in getting agriculture based services or sell their agri-produce to best buyers (customers) across the country. It brings to you the biggest farmers market.

Krishlal is a platform for Indian farmer's (Atmanirbhar Kisan and Krishi) that provide the information of farming like Mandi rates, weather information, Government (govt) schemes (Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)/ Har Khet ko Pani "Prime Minister Krishi Sinchayee Yojana", Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY), Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY), Gramin Bhandaran Yojna).

On KrishLal app, agriculture shops (Dukaan) are registered from across the country. So you can get contact details of the seller and directly contact the seller (Khad beej bhandaar/bhandar) of fertilizer (Urea, DAP, Zinc), manure (Gobar ki Khad, Cow Dung, Organic khad ), seeds (Gehun, Sarson, Bajra, Dhaan, Ganna, Kapas, Chaval ), pesticides (DDT, Super Killer, foster), etc.

KrishLal App proves to be very useful for farmers in every step such as field preparation (Khet ki tyari), Sowing (Bijai), complete crop cycle management (sowing to cultivation, Kheti ke pure season ka hisab - kitab), post-harvest crop storage (gehun , allu, payaj, tamater etc ko store karna), and sell of produce (Gehun, Bajra, Dhaan, Sarson, Chaval, Ganna). Farmers get info of all crops (Rabbi and kharib) for complete cycle from sowing to cultivation.

Krishlal empowers farmers with right data, information, products and home delivery services which make farming intelligent and easy. It gives you the product description about the agricultural practices by the best experts and specialists.

Farmer can choose from the best verified service providers/buyers based on their rate, distance, and user ratings. Farmers are fully independent to sell/buy their produce as well as give or take services from anyone of their choice.

Platform supports a variety of services starting from cultivation, crop management, sowing, spray, irrigation, harvesting and then helping to find best buyers to sell their agri-produce.

Along with connecting farmer to farmer, KrishLal help in managing service bookings, payments received from customers, payment due reminders and reports of their placed order, placed bookings, received order and received bookings.

Go digital - Simple and easy user interface for easy navigation. On the KrishLal Facebook page and YouTube channels, users can see agriculture videos and important farming (Kheti ‐ Badi ) posts.
